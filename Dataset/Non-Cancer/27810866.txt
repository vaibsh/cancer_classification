Title: Cutaneous and Visceral Chronic Granulomatous Disease Triggered by a Rubella Virus Vaccine Strain in Children With Primary Immunodeficiencies.
Abstract: Persistence of rubella live vaccine has been associated with chronic skin granuloma in 3 children with primary immunodeficiency. We describe 6 additional children with these findings, including 1 with visceral extension to the spleen.
